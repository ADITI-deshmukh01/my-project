About the T&P Cell

We bridge academia and industry to empower students with career readiness through training, internships, placements, and higher studies guidance.

Our Mission

To maximize student success by building strong industry partnerships and providing comprehensive career services and mentorship.

Our Vision

To be a nationally recognized center for career excellence, fostering innovation, inclusivity, and lifelong learning.

What We Do

Training programs, resume clinics, mock interviews, placement drives, internships, alumni connects, and postgraduate guidance.
