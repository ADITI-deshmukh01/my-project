Our mission, vision, and values

Clear goals keep us focused on what matters — your next step.

Mission

To develop technically and academically sound women professionals, having strong work-ethics and good human-being characteristics.

Vision

Empowerment and enrichment of women professionals through education and innovative training of life skills and job skills to contribute to society and nation-building.

Values

Respect for all students and staff, professionalism with good human values, team spirit, and healthy competition.
